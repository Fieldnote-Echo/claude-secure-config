# Task Protocol

Non-prescriptive planning scaffold. Generic. Stateless. Apply to any non-trivial task.

## Preflight

- What is the objective?
- What assumptions am I making?
- What principles govern this domain?

## Strategy

- What approach fits the constraints?
- What fails first?
- What am I not seeing?
- What skills and tools are available to me that serve grounded empiricism and robust correctness, even if they delay task completion?

## Execution Review

- Did the output match the objective?
- What was unexpected?
- What carries forward?
